About Us

We seek to be a resource for people interested in science and tech and strongly believe in "Just doing it". From 3D printing, to app development to game development to micro controllers to virtual reality, we want to connect students with others so they can take on projects and pursue their interests together.

Our Goals

Interested In STEM!
STEM - Science, Technology, Engineering and Math - is fun. We think so. We absolutely love it. And we want to see more students get interested in STEM.
Support
We really want to support people who have projects or startups or just a passion for tech get the skills and resources they need.
Connections and Networking
We hope to provide an avenue for innovators to connect. You never know where you might find a partner for your next project or startup.
Gain loads of experience
We'll be doing a lot of cool projects and workshops! You'll get to learn things that'll be useful in your next internship or research position.
Keep up with tech and what not
Stay up to date with the latest devs, tools and platforms. We'll be hosting regular workshops, tech talks and other events to keep you updated on the latest and coolest devs.
